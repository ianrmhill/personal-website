After 13 years, Ian continues to practice the classical guitar and is beginning to work on some original compositions.

Recordings:

Farewell by Sergio Assad

Remembrance by Sergio Assad

Milonga by Maximo Diego Pujol

In high school Ian was heavily involved in the music department, playing in the symphonic band, jazz band, jazz combo, and chamber choir ensembles. These ensembles went to a number of festivals, and Ian got to go to locations such as Banff, Seattle, and Loon Lake for performances, and to mentor younger students.

Some of the music awards Ian has received include the Darren Paul Jacobsen scholarship for excellence in music from Point Grey Secondary, earning second place in Vancouver at the Kiwanis youth guitar competition in 2014, and first in the Kiwanis jazz ensemble division with the Point Grey Jazz Band A in the same year. Recently, Coastal Jazz produced a professional recording of this jazz band, a video spotlight can be found here.
